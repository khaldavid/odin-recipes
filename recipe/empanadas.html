Empanas Colombianas

Description

An empanada is a thin bread dough, shortcrust pastry or puff pastry filled with a salty or sweet preparation and baked or fried. The filling can include red or white meat, fish, vegetables or fruit.

Ingredients

  • made with savory yellow corn dough
  • stuffed with seasoned pork
  • beef mixed with cooked potatoes

Steps to get crunchy!

  1. Vegetable oil for frying
  2. 1 ½ cups precooked yellow cornmeal
  3. 2 cups water
  4. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  5. ½ tablespoon sazon Goya with azafran
  6. ½ teaspoon Salt

    7. filling

  7. 2 cups peeled and diced white potatoes
  8. 1 chicken or vegetable bouillon tablet
  9. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  10. ¼ cup chopped white onions
  11. 1 cup chopped tomato
  12. ½ teaspoon salt
  13. ¼ cup chopped green onions
  14. 1 chopped garlic clove
  15. 2 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  16. 2 tablespoon chopped red bell pepper
  17. ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  18. ½ pound ground pork and beef
